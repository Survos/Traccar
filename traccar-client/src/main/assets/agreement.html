ABOUT THE STUDY

WHAT IS INFORMED CONSENT?

The purpose of this page is to help you decide if you want to be in the study. Please scroll through and read this entire page carefully. To join a research study you must give your informed consent. “Informed consent” includes [1] reading this page and [2] asking questions about anything that is not clear to you. You should not join this study until all of your questions are answered.

STUDY DESCRIPTION

The purpose of this study is to help researchers understand the way advertising and the retail environment affect the decisions people make about their health.

  • The study researchers will measure when and how often people come into contact with retail stores.
  • Participants will work with the study researchers to track their location during the study period by using a smartphone app that they will download on their iPhone or Android phone. The app will need to be turned on during the entire study period. Study staff may call, text, or email you to remind you to keep the app turned on.
  • We cannot guarantee that the TeliTrax app will work on all iPhone or Android models.
  • You will be asked to provide your phone number and email address so that study researchers can contact you to resolve any technical issues with the app, or to remind you to keep the app turned on. Your name and contact information will never be linked to the location information collected by your app.
  • The location information collected from your smartphone app will be linked to your responses to the CHAT surveys you have completed. This information will be linked using a confidential code; never with your name or other identifying information.
  • Incentives for participating include:
    • $30 Amazon gift card for downloading the smartphone app
    • $50 Amazon gift card monthly drawing
      • Participants are eligible for entry in the drawing if they have kept the smartphone tracking app turned on for at least 8 hours a day for at least 24 days per month.
      • There will be 10 winners per month.
      • Winners will be randomly selected during the first week of every month and will be notified by email.
      • All eligible participants have equal chance of winning.
      • Participants are not required to pay anything for entry in the drawing.
      • Should a participant win the drawing more than 7 times in one year, he/she will receive a $35 gift card instead of a $50 gift card for any drawing won during the remainder of that year. This reduced incentive it due to IRS tax regulations.

RISKS AND BENEFITS

  • The main risks if you participate in the study are the risks of loss of privacy and confidentiality.
  • The privacy policy you accepted when agreeing to join the CHAT study does not apply to TeliTrax.
  • Confidentiality of data will be maintained numerically by coding all data and disguising identifying information.
  • The risk of your information being seen is extremely low because of strong computer‑based security systems.
  • Participants may benefit from increased awareness of the way marketing affects their decision-making about their health.

You will be told about any new information that might change your decision to be in this study.

VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION AND WITHDRAWAL AND SHARING OF INFORMATION

  • Your participation in this study is voluntary. There is no penalty or loss of a benefit to which you are otherwise entitled, and you may decide not to participate or you may leave the study at any time. You do this by contacting the study researchers at (202) 525-7848.
  • Your decision to participate in TeliTrax will not affect your participation in CHAT study activities.
  • When you withdraw from the study, no information identifying you will be gathered after that date. Information that has already been gathered may still be used.
  • When the results of this study are made public, you will not be identified personally.
  • After the research is over you may review or copy your information.
  • If you have any questions, complaints or concerns while in the study, you may call the study at (202) 525-7848.
